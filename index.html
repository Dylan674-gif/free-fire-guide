PC VS MOBILE

Understanding the platform differences

Why PC Players Have Advantage

1. Mouse Precision

Mouse offers pixel-perfect accuracy with DPI adjustments (400-1600 DPI optimal). No finger friction or screen limitations.

2. Higher Refresh Rates

PC emulators support 144Hz/240Hz monitors vs 60Hz/120Hz mobile. Smoother tracking and faster reaction times.

3. No Input Lag

Direct USB polling (1000Hz) vs touch latency. PC players can react 20-40ms faster in close combat.

4. Macro & Keybinds

Custom key mapping allows instant weapon swaps, prone-shooting, and advanced movement techniques.

How Mobile Can Compete

1. Gyroscope Mastery

Use gyro + touch for hybrid aiming. Allows micro-adjustments similar to mouse lift-offs. Practice 360¬∞ spins.

Pro Tip: 300-400% gyro sensitivity

2. Close Range Combat

Mobile auto-aim assist is stronger at <15m. Rush PC players with MP40/M1887 to negate their range advantage.

Use: Chrono + M1887

3. Peek Shooting

Use left/right peek buttons. Harder for PC players to track constant ADAD movement than static targets.

Enable: Peek & Fire

4. Gloo Wall Spam

Mobile quick-gloo buttons allow faster defensive plays. PC players must press keys; you tap screen.

Equip: 6+ Gloo Walls

Emulator Matchmaking System

Garena uses advanced detection to separate PC and mobile players. Using bypass tools results in permanent bans. However, some "hybrid" devices like tablets may be matched with low-spec PCs.
